Some facts about giraffes:

  • Giraffe has extremely long neck and legs;
  • And horn-like ossicones;
  • And distinctive coat patterns;
  • Giraffes usually inhabit savannahs and woodlands;
  • Their food source is leaves, fruits and flowers of woody plants, primarily acacia species, which they browse at heights most other herbivores cannot reach.
  • Males establish social hierarchies through "necking";
  • The giraffe's prehensile tongue is about 45 cm (18 in) long.
